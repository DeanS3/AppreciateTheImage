FAQs

What are your prices/fees?

Prices are set on an individual basis depending on the needs of the customer. All charges will be itemized and agreed upon before contract signing takes place.

Is consultation 100% free?

Yes, customers are provided one free of charge consulting pertaining to specific details of the event. After the initial consultations Appreciate The Image should have enough information to move to the contract phase with the customer.

Is the website contract a binding contract?

No, the contract on our website is a generic example that covers all aspects of our services. Individual customers will get unique contracts that include details on the agreeements made between them and Appreciate The Image for their event.

Do you provide photography services?

We have freelance photographers that we can put customers in contact with if they so desire that service.
